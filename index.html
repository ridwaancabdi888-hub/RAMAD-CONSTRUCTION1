Who We Are

About RAMAD
Construction Company

RAMAD Construction Company is a modern construction and real estate company based in Hargeisa, Somaliland. The company specializes in construction services, property development, architectural solutions, and modern building projects with high-quality standards and innovative designs.

We bring international-grade construction expertise to Somaliland, combining contemporary building techniques with a deep understanding of local needs and aesthetics. Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to excellence, durability, and client satisfaction.
